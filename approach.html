Meet Her Where She Is

We believe that before society defines a girl by tribe, religion, or circumstance — she is first human. That belief drives everything we do.

Our work follows a simple principle: address the whole girl — her mind, her health, her hands, and her environment — at the same time. We don't wait for perfect conditions. We show up in classrooms, communities, and camps with what we have — volunteers, partnerships, and compassion — to remove barriers and unlock potential.

Because when you educate a girl child, you change her world — and she changes ours.

The Urgent Reality for Girls Today

These statistics underscore the profound challenges girls face globally, particularly in fragile settings, highlighting why our targeted approach is critical.

school

Girls aged 15-19 are twice as likely as boys to not be in education, employment or training. In fragile contexts, they're nearly 90% more likely to be out of school.

health_and_safety

Nearly 1 in 4 girls aged 15-19 who have ever been in a relationship have experienced intimate partner violence.

family_restroom

1 in 5 young women aged 20-24 were married as children. In fragile settings, child marriage rates are almost double the global average.

warning

Crisis, conflict and displacement all increase the risk of child marriage, sexual violence and maternal mortality for girls.

psychology

In the last 20 years, self-harm has been a leading cause of death among adolescent girls.
