Our Identity

Empowering the Potential of Every Girl.

Girls Advancement Foundation (GAF) is an indigenous NGO committed to breaking barriers for girls and women through education, health, economic empowerment, and climate-smart solutions using a multi-prong approach. We create opportunities that unlock potential, turning vulnerable lives into stories of strength, leadership, and lasting impact.

We believe that before society defines us by tribe, religion, or circumstance, we are first human. Guided by this truth, we work beyond barriers—meeting girls and women where their real needs lie, not where we assume they are.

We are more than an organization—we are a movement of hope and action, determined to unlock the potential of girls and women so they can drive change for themselves, their families, and our nation.

Our Footprint of Change

We focus our operations where the need is most acute, currently operating extensively across Central Nigeria. Our regional hubs allow us to provide localized support and monitor impact directly.
